#+title: Shui Zhu Mushrooms

Adapted from [[https://www.chinasichuanfood.com/shui-zhu-mushrooms/][this recipe]].

- 4 tbsp. vegetable oil
- 2 tbsp. doubajiang (or broad bean paste with chili powder, in a pinch)
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 2 scallions, chopped
- 1" ginger, chopped
- 3 c. vegetable broth
- 1 tbsp. Sichuan peppercorns, ground
- 1 tbsp. soy sauce
- ¼ tsp. sugar
- ½ lb. mixed mushrooms, cleaned and chopped
- 1 lb. firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 1 c. bean sprouts

Fry oil, doubijiang, garlic, scallions, and ginger in a wok for a couple minutes
until it turns bright red.

Add vegetable broth, ground Sichuan peppercorns, soy sauce, and sugar. Simmer
for five minutes or so to let the flavors combine.

Add mushrooms, tofu, and bean sprouts. Simmer for another few minutes.
