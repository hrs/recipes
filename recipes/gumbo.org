#+title: Gumbo

Adapted from Veganomicon.

- 1/3 cup of butter
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 stalks of celery
- 2 medium-size onions, diced
- 2 green bell peppers, diced
- garlic powder
- 1 (10-ounce) package frozen okra
- 1 (28-ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice, prerably fire-roated tomatoes
- 3 roasted red peppers
- 1 (16-ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 3 cups vegetable broth
- 3 tbsp tomato paste
- oregano
- all-spice
- liquid smoke
- bay leaves
- thyme
- cayenne
- salt and pepper to taste

Make roux with butter and flour. Add chopped onions and peppers to roux and cook until soft.

Add celery, garlic, okra and cook for 6 minutes. Add tomatoes, roasted red peppers, kidney beans, and vegetable broth.

Add tomato paste, liquid smoke, bay leaves, and thyme. Raise temperature and bring to gentle boil. Simmer for 35 minutes and stirring occasionally, until okra is very tender.

Cool before serving. Season with salt, pepper, and cayenne.
