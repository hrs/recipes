#+title: Gumbo

Adapted from /The Veganomicon/.

- ½ c. of butter
- ¼ c. all-purpose flour
- 4 stalks celery
- 2 medium-size onions, diced
- 2 green bell peppers, diced
- garlic powder
- 1 (10-ounce) package frozen okra
- 1 (28-ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice, preferably fire-roasted
- 3 roasted red peppers
- 1 (16-ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 3 c. vegetable broth
- all-spice
- liquid smoke
- bay leaves
- thyme
- cayenne
- salt and pepper to taste

Make roux with butter and flour. Add chopped onions and peppers to roux and cook
until soft.

Add celery, garlic, okra and cook for 6 minutes. Add tomatoes, roasted red
peppers, kidney beans, and vegetable broth.

Add liquid smoke, bay leaves, and thyme. Raise temperature and bring to gentle
boil. Simmer for 35 minutes, stirring occasionally, until okra is very tender.

Cool before serving. Season with salt, pepper, and cayenne.
