#+title: Dal Makhani

Adapted from [[https://myfoodstory.com/dal-makhani-recipe/][My Food Story]].

- 1 c. black dal
- 1 can kidney beans
- 1 stick of butter
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 green chili or serrano peppers, diced
- 4 tomatoes, diced
- 1 bay leaf
- cinnamon
- cloves
- cardamom
- salt
- chili powder
- garam masala
- 1 c. heavy cream or half-and-half
- 1 tbsp. liquid smoke
- 4 c. of water
- minced ginger paste
- garlic powder or minced garlic

Soak dried dal overnight for at least 8 hours. Remove any tiny stones.

Drain the water and cook dal and beans on stove top with water and some salt.
Cook for an hour until lentils are very soft.

Heat half of stick of butter in a pot and add cinnamon, cloves, and cardamom
until fragrant.

Add garlic, ginger paste, onions, green chili, tomatoes, garam masala, salt, and
chili powder and cook until soft.

Add cooked lentils, bay leaf, remaining butter, and heavy cream. Stir to ensure
nothing is sticking to pan.
