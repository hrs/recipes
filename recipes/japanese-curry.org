#+title: Japanese Curry

Adapted from [[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QhsrBz9Cceg][Kenji Lopez Alt]].

- 1 bag of small potatoes, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 turnip, diced (optional)
- 1 zucchini, diced
- 1 apple, diced
- 3 tbsp. curry powder
- 1 tbsp. garam masala
- salt
- 2 tbsp. of honey or BBQ sauce (optional)
- 3 tbsp. flour
- 4 c. vegetable broth
- vegetable oil

Add onion to pot with vegetable oil, curry powder, garam masala, and flour. Cook
until flour is cooked and onions are light brown.

Add broth and wait for simmer.

Add potatoes, carrot, and turnip and cook on high for 30 min.

Stir in BBQ sauce.

Add zucchini and apples. Cook on high for 15 min.

Serve with rice.
